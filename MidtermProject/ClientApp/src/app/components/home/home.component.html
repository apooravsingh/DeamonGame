The Devil's Apprentice

Welcome to Hell!

- To win this game you need to beat the Devil's apprentice, Reynard.

- He is known for deceiving others for his own advantage.

- To win the game, you must beat Reynard in his dice challenge.

- In each turn, the you can roll dice as many times as you wish

- Each result of the dice will get added to your CURRENT SCORE.

- BUT, if you roll a 1,the CURRENT SCORE is lost. After that, it's Reynard's turn

- You can choose to 'Hold', which means that your CURRENT score gets added to your TOTAL score.

- The only way to win the game is to reach 100 points before Reynard!!
