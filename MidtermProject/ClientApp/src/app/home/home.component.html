Beat The Deamon!

Instructions

The rules of the game

- The game can have as may players as you choose, playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to the player's ROUND score

- BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score beats the deamon!

- The number of times you need to beat the deamon for good depends on the strength of your team

BEST OF LUCK!
